About Myself

Skills:

I specialize in user-centered design, focusing on creating intuitive interfaces. Proficient in Figma, Adobe XD, and Sketch, I excel at wireframing, prototyping, and user testing. My experience with design systems ensures consistency across digital platforms.

My journey in Design:

I discovered my passion for design 2 years ago, focusing on solving problems through creative solutions. My journey has allowed me to work on diverse projects, continually refining my skills in UI/UX.

Interest:

I’m passionate about exploring the intersection of technology and human behavior, delving into psychology and product development. When I'm not designing, I enjoy finding inspiration in nature.

Digital Experience:

I’ve designed various digital products, including websites and mobile apps, prioritizing user-first approaches. My focus is on creating intuitive flows and engaging interfaces through research and iterative design.
